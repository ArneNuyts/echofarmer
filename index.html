About

echofarmer (BE) is the alias of Brussels-based producer and musician Arne Nuyts. With a sound as suited for the club as for the couch, echofarmer crafts enchanting miniverses. Hyperdetailed percussion and deep subs fuse with vocal-like synths, acoustic instruments, and found sounds. Combining childlike joy with surgical precision, every live set is infectiously unique, all unified by echofarmer's tactile approach to sound and relentless appetite for experimentation.

From teletubbie hyperjungle to bedroomstep and pillowcore, echofarmer blurs the line between dance floor and blanket fort.
