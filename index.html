About

Hi! I'm echofarmer (all lower case please), a Brussels-based producer and musician. For each club show in the past two years I compulsively created new tracks. As I kept refining them through playing live, an entity took shape.
Tiny Big Much became a mixtape that celebrates my deep connection with the computer as an instrument. As new parts of Tiny Big Much are released, this website grows along. Crafted with love by me, I hope you enjoy this evolving miniverse.
