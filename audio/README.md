# Audio Files

Place your audio samples here.

## Recommended Formats:
- **MP3**: Best compatibility (128-192 kbps)
- **WAV**: Highest quality but larger files
- **OGG**: Good compression, wide support

## File Naming:
Match the filenames in `script.js` configuration:
- sample1.mp3
- sample2.mp3
- sample3.mp3
- sample4.mp3
- etc.

## Tips:
- Keep files under 1MB for fast loading
- Use short loops (1-4 seconds) for a sampler effect
- Normalize audio levels for consistent playback
- Consider crossfading or looping for musical samples

## Recommended Audio Samples:
- Drum loops
- Bass lines
- Synth patterns
- Vocal snippets
- Sound effects
- Musical phrases

The audio will play instantly when hovering (desktop) or tapping (mobile) the corresponding GIF!
